About Me

I am a dedicated biostatistician with a passion for transforming complex healthcare data into actionable insights that improve public health outcomes. My journey began with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery, where I honed my clinical and research skills. Driven by my interest in understanding health disparities, I pursued a Master’s in Public Health with a concentration in Epidemiology and Biostatistics.

Throughout my career, I have worked on diverse projects, including quality improvement studies, clinical research, and predictive modeling. I’ve collaborated with multidisciplinary teams to analyze complex datasets, develop statistical models, and contribute to impactful research. My technical expertise spans advanced tools like R, SAS, Python, and SQL, which I’ve used to manage and analyze healthcare data, ensuring accuracy and precision.

My professional ethos is rooted in curiosity and collaboration, and I thrive on using data to uncover trends, identify risk factors, and recommend evidence-based solutions. Whether working on clinical trials, epidemiological studies, or public health initiatives, I aim to make a meaningful difference in healthcare through data-driven strategies.
